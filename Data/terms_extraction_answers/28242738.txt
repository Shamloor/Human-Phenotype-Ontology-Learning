1. Fixational eye movements  
2. Fixational eye movements are small, involuntary eye movements that occur during visual fixation, including microsaccades, ocular drift, and ocular tremor, and are controlled by the same neuronal mechanisms as larger saccades.  
3. hasExactSynonym: fixational eye movements; microsaccades  
   hasRelatedSynonym: ocular drift; ocular tremor
