1. Dys-megakaryopoiesis  
2. Dys-megakaryopoiesis is a pathological condition characterized by abnormal megakaryocyte development, including the presence of micro-megakaryocytes and dysplastic megakaryocytes, identified through CD41 immune staining and morphological analysis in myelodysplastic syndromes.
