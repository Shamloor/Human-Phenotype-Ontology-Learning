1. Hyperbiliverdinaemia  
2. A medical condition characterized by elevated levels of biliverdin in the blood, often resulting from a genetic defect in the biliverdin reductase-A (BVR-A) gene combined with liver dysfunction, and presenting clinically as green jaundice.
