1. Varicella-Zoster Virus (VZV) infection  
2. A viral infection caused by the Varicella-Zoster Virus, characterized by primary infection leading to varicella (chickenpox) and potential reactivation causing herpes zoster (shingles), with associated neurological complications.  
3. hasExactSynonym: VZV infection  
   hasRelatedSynonym: herpes zoster; chickenpox  
   hasNarrowSynonym: postherpetic neuralgia; VZV vasculopathy; VZV encephalitis
